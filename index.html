Frequently Asked Questions

The questions we’re asked most often by homeowners looking to sell fast in Glasgow.

How quickly can you buy my house in Glasgow?

We can move quickly if you need speed. In many cases, completion can happen within weeks depending on solicitor timescales and the information available.

Do you charge any fees?

No. We don’t charge estate agent fees or commissions. Your solicitor may still charge legal fees, as with any sale.

Will you buy a property with damp or major repairs needed?

Yes. We consider properties in all conditions, including damp, dated interiors, structural issues, and homes requiring refurbishment.

Is your offer obligation-free?

Yes. Our offer is no-obligation. You can compare options and decide what’s right for you.

Do I need a Home Report for a direct cash sale?

Not always. Many off-market sales proceed without a Home Report requirement, depending on the route and solicitor advice. We’ll explain what’s typical and what your solicitor may recommend.
