Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you buy my house?

We can move as fast as you need. In many cases, we can exchange contracts within 7-28 days, depending on the solicitors.

Do you charge any fees?

No. Unlike estate agents who charge a percentage commission, we charge zero fees. The cash offer we make is the amount you receive.

My house needs major repairs, will you still buy it?

Yes. We buy properties in any condition—whether it’s damp, dated, or structurally damaged. You don't need to decorate or fix anything.

Is the offer obligated?

No, our cash offers are 100% no-obligation. You are free to accept or decline.
