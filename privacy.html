Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At SB Properties UK ("we", "us", "our"), we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide to us through our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Identity Data: First name and last name.
  • Contact Data: Email address and telephone number.
  • Property Data: Address, postcode, and details about the property you wish to sell.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To contact you regarding your enquiry and provide a property valuation or cash offer.
  • To arrange viewings or discussions about your property.
  • To communicate with you throughout the sales process.

We do not use your data for marketing newsletters unless you explicitly opt-in.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share your information with trusted professional partners (such as solicitors or surveyors) only if it is necessary to progress your sale, and we will always inform you before doing so.

4. Data Security

We have implemented security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorised way. Access to your personal data is limited to those employees and partners who have a business need to know.

5. Your Legal Rights (GDPR)

Under data protection laws, you have rights including:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of your personal data.
  • Request erasure of your personal data.
  • Object to processing of your personal data.
